MOVIES TICKETS

Select Movie and Showtime: Choose the movie, showtime, and theater you want to attend. Choose Seats: Select your preferred seats. Proceed to Payment: Enter your payment details and confirm your booking. Receive Confirmation: You will receive a booking confirmation email or SMS. M-ticket: BookMyShow uses M-tickets, which can only be accessed and displayed on a mobile phone using the BookMyShow app or mobile web. Login: M-tickets require a user to be logged in to an existing account when transacting and when using the M-ticket for entry at the venue
